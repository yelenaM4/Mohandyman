Types of housekeeper


Cleaner or Maid

A basic cleaning service is provided by a cleaner or maid. A housekeeper is not to be mistaken with a maid. They might be utilized in larger commercial cleaning locations, or for end-of-tenancy cleanings. They may be used for brief visits in private houses, and they might be scheduled on a regular basis, but they are seldom employed full-time for a single property.


Plumber

Plumbers install and repair pipes and fixtures that carry water, gas, or other fluids in homes and businesses. They also maintain plumbing fixtures like bathtubs and toilets and appliances such as dishwashers or heating systems.


Gardener

Gardener responsibilities include monitoring the health of all plants and greenscapes, watering and feeding plants, trimming trees and shrubs, fertilizing and mowing lawns, weeding gardens and keeping green spaces and walkways clear of debris and litter. You should know how to use and maintain landscaping equipment, including mowers, trimmers and fertilizers, while following health and safety regulations.


Electricians

Electricians work on electrical equipment and in homes, factories, businesses, and buildings, both existing and under construction, to install, repair, and maintain power, lighting, communications, and control systems.
